How to Make a Colorful Sun with a Red Candle and Markers

Chapter 1: Introduction – Bring the Sun Indoors!

Have you ever wished you could capture the warmth and beauty of the sun inside your home? Now you can — with just a red candle and a handful of colorful markers!

This simple and fun project is perfect for kids, teens, or anyone who loves arts and crafts. You’ll be creating a bright, vibrant sun sculpture using everyday items. Whether you want to decorate your desk, light up your room with color, or just enjoy a peaceful moment of creativity, this mini sun project is a great way to get started.

Let’s dive in!


Chapter 2: What You’ll Need & How to Set It Up

Materials Needed:

  • 1 red candle (pillar or jar-style works best)
  • 8 to 12 colorful markers (the more colors, the better!)
  • A clean, flat surface like a table or desk
  • A round plate or piece of cardboard (optional, for easier setup)

Step-by-Step Instructions:

1.   Place the Candle:
Set your red candle in the center of your surface. The red candle represents the fiery heart of the sun.

2.   Choose Your Markers:
Pick a variety of marker colors — yellows, oranges, blues, purples, greens — to represent rays of light. Try to include warm tones to mimic sunlight, and cool tones for artistic contrast.

3.   Create the Sun Shape:
Arrange your markers around the candle in a circular pattern, like rays stretching out from the center of the sun. Make sure the tips of the markers are pointing outward, and the bottoms are close to the base of the candle.

4.   Adjust the Rays:
Space the markers evenly so the "sun rays" look symmetrical. You can angle them slightly for a dynamic look, or keep them perfectly straight for a clean, geometric feel.


Chapter 3: Enjoy Your Mini Sun & Make It Your Own

Now that your colorful sun is assembled, take a step back and admire your creation! You’ve just turned ordinary art supplies into a glowing, cheerful centerpiece.

Ideas to Customize Your Sun:

  • Light the Candle (with adult supervision if you're young!) and watch how the flickering flame adds life to your sun.
  • Add Decorations: Use yellow tissue paper or glitter around the base to add more sparkle.
  • Switch It Up: Use highlighters, crayons, or even colored pencils for different styles.
  • Snap a Photo: Share your sun with friends or on social media to inspire others.

This project reminds us that with a little imagination, even simple objects can shine bright. You’ve made more than just a sun — you’ve made a moment of joy.
