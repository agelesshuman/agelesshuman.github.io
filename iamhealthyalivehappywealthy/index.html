Chapter 1: I AM HEALTHY

Health is the foundation of every dream we pursue. When I say “I am healthy,” I am not just referring to the state of my body, but also the clarity of my mind and the peace of my spirit. Health begins in the mind—with every thought I think and every word I speak.

I choose nourishment. I eat foods that fuel my energy and strengthen my body. I move my body every day, honoring it through activity, breath, and stillness. I sleep deeply, and I wake with gratitude.

I am grateful for every heartbeat, every breath, every step. My body knows how to heal, and I trust it completely. Even when I face challenges, I remind myself: I am healing. I am growing stronger. I am healthy.


Chapter 2: I AM ALIVE & I AM HAPPY

To be alive is a miracle. It is easy to forget this truth in the busyness of everyday life. But when I pause and breathe deeply, I reconnect with the wonder of existence. I feel my heart beating. I feel the air on my skin. I feel the life force within me.

I am not just existing—I am truly living. I create experiences. I make memories. I touch lives and allow others to touch mine. I laugh, I cry, I love, I learn. All of this is part of being alive.

And from this place of aliveness, happiness blooms. I do not wait for the perfect moment to be happy. I choose happiness now. I find joy in the small things—a smile, a kind word, a sunrise. I cultivate a spirit of gratitude, and with every thankful thought, my happiness expands.

I am alive. I am happy. And that is enough.


Chapter 3: I AM WEALTHY

Wealth is more than numbers in a bank account. It is the richness of life itself. I am wealthy in love, in ideas, in potential. I am wealthy in time, in energy, in the ability to create, share, and grow.

Every day, I recognize the abundance around me. Opportunities flow to me effortlessly. I am aligned with my purpose, and I attract success naturally. Money comes to me in expected and unexpected ways, and I use it wisely, generously, and joyfully.

I let go of scarcity thinking. I know there is enough. I am enough. My wealth grows because I believe in my worth and in the limitless possibilities of life.

I am healthy. I am alive. I am happy. I am wealthy.

And so it is.
